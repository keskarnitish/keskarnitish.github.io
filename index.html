Nitish Shirish Keskar

 

I'm a Principal Research Scientist at Salesforce Research in Palo Alto where I work on Deep Learning and its applications to Natural Language Processing. I am particularly interested in large language models (LLMs), efficient training methods, and issues pertaining to generalization and scalability. I am also interested in Deep Learning systems and tooling.

I received my PhD from Northwestern University in 2017 under the supervision of Prof. Jorge Nocedal and Prof. Andreas Waechter. For my PhD, I focused on efficiently finding solutions to Mathematical Optimization problems which are nonsmooth or stochastic. This includes several problems in Machine Learning and Deep Learning.
